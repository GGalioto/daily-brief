Willingness to pay — UNCLEAR ⚠️

That's a yellow flag: demand for the function ≠ demand for a paid product. The spend is on hardware, and existing tools monetize via affiliate/retail referral, not subscriptions.

Knowledge base feasibility — STRONG ✅

Specs, mil-spec standards, assembly guides, twist rates, and compatibility rules are abundant and public. Building the rules engine is very doable. This is the easiest part of the problem — which is also why others have done it.
